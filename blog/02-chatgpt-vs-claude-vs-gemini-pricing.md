## FAQ

### Is ChatGPT Plus worth $20/month?

If you use GPT-4o regularly, yes. The free tier is limited to GPT-3.5, which is significantly less capable. For casual use, the free tier may be sufficient.

### Is Claude Pro better than ChatGPT Plus?

It depends on your use case. Claude excels at long documents and coding, while ChatGPT has better plugin support and image generation. Both cost $20/month.

### Is Gemini Advanced worth it?

If you use Google Workspace, yes. Gemini Advanced integrates with Gmail, Docs, and Sheets. For standalone use, it's comparable to ChatGPT Plus and Claude Pro.

### Which API is cheapest?

Gemini 1.5 Flash is the cheapest at $0.075/1M input tokens. For higher quality, Claude 3.5 Haiku ($0.25/1M) and GPT-4o-mini ($0.15/1M) offer good value.

### Can I use multiple platforms?

Yes! Many users combine platforms:
- ChatGPT for creative tasks
- Claude for coding and analysis
- Gemini for Google integration
